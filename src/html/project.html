Building Futures: OSIM Foundation's Impactful Projects

Explore how our initiatives are transforming lives and empowering communities in Tanzania.

Empowering Girls with Sanitary Pads

OSIM Foundation provides sanitary pads to students, particularly girls, ensuring they have access to essential menstrual hygiene products. This initiative empowers female students, reduces barriers to education, and fosters a supportive learning environment.

Supporting Maternal and Infant Health

Our maternal and infant care programs ensure access to essential healthcare and nutrition for mothers and infants. We provide prenatal education, postnatal support, and guidance on breastfeeding and infant hygiene to create a nurturing environment for families.

Combating Malnutrition

OSIM Foundation addresses malnutrition through sustainable food programs and community-driven initiatives. We provide nourishing meals and educate families on proper nutrition to improve health and development in vulnerable communities.
